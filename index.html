• About Me

I’m a self-taught photographer based in Uruguay.
I started photography driven by curiosity and the need to observe everyday scenes more carefully. I’m particularly interested in light, composition, and quiet moments that often go unnoticed.

My work is mostly personal and exploratory. I use photography as a way to learn, experiment, and document how I see the world, without following rigid rules or commercial expectations.

This portfolio is a collection of ongoing projects, visual notes, and studies — a record of my process and growth over time.
